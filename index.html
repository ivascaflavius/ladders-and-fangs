How to Play

Goal: get both your tokens to square 100 exactly — first there wins.
Roll, then pick which of your 2 tokens moves. That choice is the whole game.
Land exactly on an opponent's token to send it home — unless the square is safe (ladder bottoms, snake heads, square 1).
A token on 100 is locked in for good — can't be captured or swapped.
No one to play with? Try Play vs Computer from the main menu.

Cards

Land on a card square to draw one (max 2 held):

  • Shield — automatic. Cancels a snake bite the instant it happens; doesn't stop traps.
  • Swap — play instead of rolling: swap one of your tokens with an opponent's.
  • Double Move — appears after you roll: move both tokens by the roll (or one token roll×2, if the other's already locked at 100).
  • Trap — play instead of rolling, then hide it on an empty square. An opponent landing there rolls the die and is knocked back ×5 squares (5–30) — Shield can't stop it. Stays invisible to them until it springs.
Trailing by 30+ squares? Card squares draw you two candidates to choose from instead of one at random.

About

Ladders & Fangs is a strategy twist on Snakes & Ladders — real-time peer-to-peer multiplayer, right in the browser. No accounts, no ads, no server backend beyond connecting two players. Built as a solo passion project.
